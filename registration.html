NoCaSS 2024: Code of Conduct

NoCaSS is committed to fostering a safe, inclusive, and productive atmosphere that warmly welcomes all participants. In this regard, harassment in any form cannot be tolerated and all participants are required to abide by the Code of Conduct for the event. Everyone, including the participants as well as the organisers, is expected to abide by the Code of Conduct and as such, every committee member should endeavour to re-enforce it before, during and after the Symposium. Treat others with kindness and act with respect.
